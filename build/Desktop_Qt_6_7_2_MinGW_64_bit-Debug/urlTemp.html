About

Genius Annotation

“Die With A Smile” is the comeback single by Lady Gaga and Bruno Mars, who both haven’t released music in 2 to 3 years. The song was surprise announced a day before the release on August 15, and released at 12am EST alongside the music video.

Gaga confirmed on the announcement post that this song is a song to hold fans over until she releases her highly anticipated seventh studio album, which she has teased throughout all of 2024.

What have the artists said about the song?
Genius Answer

Lady Gaga:

Bruno and I have a lot of mutual respect for each other and were talking about collaborating. I was finishing up my own album in Malibu and one night after a long day he asked me to come to his studio to hear something he was working on. It was around midnight when I got there and I was blown away when I heard what he had started making. We stayed up all night and finished writing and recording the song. Bruno’s talent is beyond explanation. His musicianship and vision is next level. There’s no one like him.

Bruno Mars:

Getting to work with Gaga has been an honor. She’s an icon and she makes this song magical. I’m so excited for everyone to hear it.

— via Variety

How was "Die With a Smile" teased by Lady Gaga and Bruno Mars?
Genius Answer

Lady Gaga started teasing the track on her Instagram with a piano teaser of the track on August 14. On August 15 Lady Gaga announced the release on her Instagram, saying: “While you wait on LG7…”, also teasing her next album.

Bruno Mars posted a picture on his Instagram wearing merchandise from Lady Gaga on August 15. A few hours later he posted the cover on his Instagram, saying: “Song and video tonight! 9pm PT”.

Have Lady Gaga and Bruno Mars ever collaborated before?
Genius Answer

No, “Die with a Smile” marks the first collaboration between the two.
