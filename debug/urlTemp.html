About

Genius Annotation

Produced entirely by YouTuber Jon Cozart (aka Paint), this song parodies Disney’s “Happily Ever After” endings by describing a realistic outcome for four famous princesses: Ariel, Jasmine, Belle, and Pocahontas.

Cozart said it took him two days of near constant filming to produce the video, and ended up redoing it after sending it to his sister and having her suggest four different backgrounds. Clearly, there was a lot of effort put into it.
